# Audit KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Internal audit performance indicators measuring assurance quality, risk coverage, and organizational impact.

## Audit KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Audit Plan Completion | 100% | Annual | CAE |
| High-Risk Area Coverage | 100% | Annual | CAE |
| Finding Remediation Rate | 95%+ on time | Monthly | Audit Mgr |
| Audit Cycle Time | Per audit type | Per audit | Audit Lead |
| Stakeholder Satisfaction | 90%+ | Annual | CAE |
| Repeat Findings | <10% | Annual | Audit Mgr |
| External Audit Reliance | Increasing | Annual | CAE |

## Audit Coverage Metrics

### Risk-Based Coverage

| Risk Rating | Audit Frequency | Coverage |
|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| Critical | Annual | 100% |
| High | 18 months | 100% |
| Medium | 24 months | 80%+ |
| Low | 36 months | 50%+ |

### Audit Universe

- Total auditable units
- Units audited this year
- Rolling coverage percentage
- Emerging risk assessments

## Finding Metrics

### Finding Distribution

| Severity | Target Distribution | Remediation SLA |
|----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Critical | <5% | 30 days |
| High | <15% | 60 days |
| Medium | <50% | 90 days |
| Low | Remainder | 180 days |

### Remediation Tracking

- Open findings by age
- Past-due findings
- Root cause patterns
- Control effectiveness improvements

## Quality Metrics

### Audit Quality

- Peer review scores
- External quality assessment (5-year cycle)
- Standards conformance rate: 100%
- Audit methodology adherence

### Team Performance

- Auditor utilization: 75%+
- Training hours per auditor
- Certification maintenance
- Knowledge sharing contributions

## Impact Metrics

| Process improvements | Tracked | Per audit |
| Control enhancements | Tracked | Per audit |
| Management action rate | 100% | Per finding |

# Technology Advisory Board KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Technology advisory board performance indicators measuring technical guidance quality, innovation direction, and technology strategy support.

## TAB KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Technology Roadmap Input | Incorporated | Quarterly | CTO |
| Emerging Tech Identification | Early insight | Quarterly | TAB Chair |
| Architecture Review Quality | High value | Per review | TAB Liaison |
| Vendor/Partner Insights | Actionable | Quarterly | TAB Liaison |
| Technical Due Diligence Support | Available | As needed | CTO |
| Innovation Pipeline Review | Strategic input | Quarterly | R&D Head |
| Tech Talent Network | Connections made | Quarterly | CTO |

### Strategic Technology Input

| Area | Metric | Tracking |
|------|--------|----------|

- Technologies evaluated
- Trend accuracy (12-month retrospective)
- Early warning signals provided
- Proof of concept recommendations

## Advisory Sessions

### Meeting Effectiveness

- Actionable recommendations per meeting
- Implementation consideration rate

# Risk Management KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Enterprise risk management performance indicators measuring risk identification, mitigation, and organizational resilience.

## Risk Management KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Risk Assessment Completion | 100% | Annual | CRO |
| Risk Mitigation Plan Adherence | 95%+ | Quarterly | Risk Mgr |
| Risk Incidents (Material) | Decreasing | Monthly | Risk Mgr |
| Loss Event Severity | Within tolerance | Per event | CRO |
| Risk Appetite Compliance | 100% | Quarterly | CRO |
| Business Continuity Test Success | 100% | Annual | BC Manager |
| Risk Culture Score | 80%+ | Annual | CRO |

## Risk Identification

### Risk Register Health

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Risks documented | 100% identified | Quarterly |
| Risk owners assigned | 100% | Ongoing |
| Risk assessments current | 100% | Quarterly |
| Emerging risks tracked | Active | Monthly |

### Risk Assessment Quality

- Consistent methodology: 100%
- Quantification where applicable
- Scenario analysis: Key risks
- Stress testing: Annual

## Risk Mitigation

### Control Effectiveness

- Key controls tested: 100%
- Control gaps identified: Documented
- Remediation on track: 95%+
- Residual risk acceptable: 100%

### Risk Treatment

| Treatment | Application | Success Rate |
|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| Avoid | Strategic decisions | Per decision |
| Transfer | Insurance/contracts | Claims ratio |
| Mitigate | Controls/actions | Effectiveness |
| Accept | Within appetite | Monitored |

## Operational Risk

### Loss Events

- Loss events logged: 100%
- Root cause analysis: 100% of significant
- Near-miss reporting: Active
- Trend analysis: Quarterly

### Key Risk Indicators (KRIs)

- KRIs defined: All key risks
- Threshold breaches: Escalated
- KRI trending: Monitored
- Early warning effectiveness

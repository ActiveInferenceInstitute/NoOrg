# Board Meeting Process

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01
**Owner:** Corporate Secretary

## Purpose

Defines the process for planning, conducting, and following up on board of directors meetings.

## Meeting Types

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Regular | Quarterly | Routine governance |
| Annual | Yearly | Annual matters, elections |
| Special | As needed | Urgent matters |
| Committee | Per charter | Specific oversight |

## Pre-Meeting Activities

### 30 Days Prior

| Activity | Owner |
|----------|-------|
| Set agenda | Chair + CEO |
| Calendar confirmation | Corporate Secretary |
| Initial materials request | Corporate Secretary |

### 14 Days Prior

| Activity | Owner |
|----------|-------|
| Materials due | Presenters |
| Legal review | General Counsel |
| Materials compilation | Corporate Secretary |

### 7 Days Prior

| Activity | Owner |
|----------|-------|
| Board package distribution | Corporate Secretary |
| Director confirmations | Corporate Secretary |
| Logistics finalization | Administrative support |

### 1 Day Prior

| Activity | Owner |
|----------|-------|
| Final logistics check | Corporate Secretary |
| Executive session prep | Chair |
| Emergency updates | CEO |

## Meeting Conduct

### Standard Agenda Structure

| Item | Duration | Lead |
|------|----------|------|
| Call to Order | 5 min | Chair |
| Minutes Approval | 10 min | Chair |
| CEO Report | 30 min | CEO |
| CFO Report | 20 min | CFO |
| Committee Reports | 30 min | Committee Chairs |
| Strategic Discussion | 45 min | Management |
| Action Items | 15 min | Chair |
| Executive Session | As needed | Chair |
| Adjournment | 5 min | Chair |

### Quorum Requirements

| Meeting Type | Quorum |
|--------------|--------|
| Regular | Majority of directors |
| Committee | Per committee charter |
| Special | Per bylaws |

### Voting Procedures

| Decision Type | Standard |
|---------------|----------|
| Routine matters | Simple majority |
| Bylaw amendments | Supermajority |
| Director elections | Majority or plurality |

## Post-Meeting Activities

### Within 48 Hours

| Activity | Owner |
|----------|-------|
| Draft minutes | Corporate Secretary |
| Action item distribution | Corporate Secretary |
| Follow-up requests | Corporate Secretary |

### Within 2 Weeks

| Activity | Owner |
|----------|-------|
| Minutes review | General Counsel |
| Director feedback | Corporate Secretary |
| Action item tracking | Responsible parties |

### Before Next Meeting

| Activity | Owner |
|----------|-------|
| Minutes approval prep | Corporate Secretary |
| Action item status | Responsible parties |
| Materials preparation | Management |

## Documentation Requirements

| Document | Retention |
|----------|-----------|
| Minutes | Permanent |
| Board packages | 7 years |
| Resolutions | Permanent |
| Attendance records | 7 years |

## Executive Session

### Purpose

- Independent director discussion
- CEO evaluation
- Compensation matters
- Sensitive issues

### Conduct

| Standard | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| Frequency | Every meeting |
| Attendance | Non-management directors only |
| Documentation | Summary notes only |
| Confidentiality | Strictly maintained |

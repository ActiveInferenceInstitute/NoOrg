# Governance Processes

This directory contains key governance processes that define how organizational governance activities are conducted, ensuring accountability, transparency, and effective decision-making.

## Core Processes

| Process | Description | Owner |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| [Decision Making Framework](./Decision_Making_Framework.md) | Structured approaches for organizational decisions, RACI/RAPID models | Governance |
| [Policy Development Process](./Policy_Development_Process.md) | End-to-end policy lifecycle from initiation to retirement | Governance |
| [Board Meeting Process](./Board_Meeting_Process.md) | Planning, conducting, and following up on board meetings | Corporate Secretary |
| [Regulatory Filing Process](./Regulatory_Filing_Process.md) | Managing regulatory filings and submissions | Compliance |
| [Governance Calendar Process](./Governance_Calendar_Process.md) | Annual governance calendar and activity planning | Corporate Secretary |

## Process Categories

### Decision Governance

- Authority matrices and delegation
- Escalation pathways
- Decision documentation requirements
- Appeal and review mechanisms

### Meeting Management

- Board and committee meetings
- Annual shareholder meeting
- Executive sessions
- Special meetings

### Policy Management

- Policy development lifecycle
- Policy review and updates
- Exception management
- Retirement process

### Compliance and Reporting

- Regulatory filing calendar
- Disclosure management
- Compliance monitoring
- Governance reporting

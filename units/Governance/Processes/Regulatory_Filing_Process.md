# Regulatory Filing Process

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01
**Owner:** Chief Compliance Officer

## Purpose

Defines the process for managing regulatory filings and submissions across all jurisdictions.

## Filing Categories

| Category | Examples | Frequency |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Financial | SEC 10-K, 10-Q, 8-K | Quarterly/Annual/Event |
| Tax | Federal, state, international | Annual |
| Employment | EEO, wage reports | Annual |
| Industry | Sector-specific filings | Varies |
| Corporate | State filings, annual reports | Annual |

## Filing Calendar

### Annual Filings

| Filing | Due Date | Owner |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Annual Report (10-K) | 60 days after FYE | Finance + Legal |
| Proxy Statement | 120 days before meeting | Legal |
| State annual reports | Per state | Legal |
| Tax returns | Extension: 6 months | Tax |

### Quarterly Filings

| Filing | Due Date | Owner |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Quarterly Report (10-Q) | 40 days after QE | Finance + Legal |
| Beneficial ownership | 45 days after QE | Legal |
| Estimated taxes | 15th of quarter month | Tax |

### Event-Driven Filings

| Event | Filing | Timeline |
|-------|--------|----------|
| Material event | 8-K | 4 business days |
| Insider transaction | Form 4 | 2 business days |
| 5% ownership | Schedule 13D/G | 10 days |
| Executive change | 8-K | 4 business days |

## Filing Process

### Phase 1: Preparation

| Activity | Timeline | Owner |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Gather data | T-30 days | Departments |
| Draft content | T-20 days | Lead owners |
| Initial review | T-14 days | Internal reviewers |
| Management review | T-10 days | Senior management |

### Phase 2: Review

| Review Level | Timeline | Reviewer |
|--------------|----------|----------|
| Legal review | T-7 days | General Counsel |
| Audit committee | T-5 days | Audit Committee |
| External review | T-3 days | External counsel/auditor |
| Final approval | T-1 day | CEO/CFO |

### Phase 3: Submission

| Activity | Owner |
|----------|-------|
| Filing preparation | Compliance |
| System submission | Compliance |
| Confirmation | Compliance |
| Documentation | Compliance |

### Phase 4: Post-Filing

| Activity | Timeline | Owner |
|----------|----------|-------|
| Filing confirmation | Same day | Compliance |
| Website posting | Same day | Communications |
| Record retention | Ongoing | Records management |
| Lessons learned | Within 1 week | Process owner |

## Quality Controls

### Review Checkpoints

| Checkpoint | Verification |
|------------|--------------|
| Data accuracy | Source verification |
| Completeness | Checklist review |
| Consistency | Cross-reference check |
| Formatting | Standard compliance |
| Signature | Authorization confirmed |

### Error Prevention

| Control | Implementation |
|---------|----------------|
| Dual review | Two-person review |
| Checklists | Standard checklists |
| System validation | Automated checks |
| Version control | Change tracking |

## Escalation

| Issue | Escalation Path |
|-------|-----------------|
| Deadline risk | CCO → CFO → CEO |
| Content dispute | Legal → GC → CEO |
| Data unavailable | Owner → VP → CFO |
| Filing error | Immediate → CCO → GC |

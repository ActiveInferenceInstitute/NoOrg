# Governance Calendar Process

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01
**Owner:** Corporate Secretary

## Purpose

Defines the process for managing the annual governance calendar and ensuring timely completion of all governance activities.

## Annual Governance Calendar

### Q1 Activities

| Month | Activity | Owner |
|-------|----------|-------|
| January | Annual audit planning | Audit Committee |
| January | Compensation committee - annual review | Compensation Committee |
| February | Annual report preparation | Finance + Legal |
| February | Proxy preparation begins | Legal |
| March | Board risk assessment | Risk Committee |
| March | Year-end audit completion | Audit Committee |

### Q2 Activities

| Month | Activity | Owner |
|-------|----------|-------|
| April | Annual meeting preparation | Corporate Secretary |
| April | Director nominations | Nominating Committee |
| May | Annual shareholder meeting | Board |
| May | Committee appointments | Board |
| June | Board education | Corporate Secretary |
| June | Quarterly business review | Board |

### Q3 Activities

| Month | Activity | Owner |
|-------|----------|-------|
| July | Strategic planning kick-off | CEO + Board |
| July | Mid-year governance review | Governance Committee |
| August | Executive compensation review | Compensation Committee |
| August | Succession planning review | Compensation Committee |
| September | Strategic plan approval | Board |
| September | Quarterly business review | Board |

### Q4 Activities

| Month | Activity | Owner |
|-------|----------|-------|
| October | Budget presentation | CFO |
| October | Annual policy review | Governance |
| November | Board self-assessment | Lead Director |
| November | Director nominations begins | Nominating Committee |
| December | Budget approval | Board |
| December | Quarterly business review | Board |

## Calendar Management Process

### Annual Planning

| Timing | Activity | Owner |
|--------|----------|-------|
| November | Draft next year calendar | Corporate Secretary |
| December | Leadership review | CEO + Chair |
| December | Board approval | Board |
| January | Calendar publication | Corporate Secretary |

### Ongoing Management

| Activity | Frequency | Owner |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Calendar updates | As needed | Corporate Secretary |
| Reminder notices | Monthly | Corporate Secretary |
| Deadline tracking | Weekly | Corporate Secretary |
| Status reporting | Monthly | Corporate Secretary |

## Meeting Scheduling

### Board Meetings

| Meeting | Standard Timing |
|---------|-----------------|
| Q1 Board | Late February/Early March |
| Annual Meeting | May |
| Q2 Board | June |
| Strategic Planning | July-August |
| Q3 Board | September |
| Q4 Board | November-December |

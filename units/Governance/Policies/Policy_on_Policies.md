# Policy on Policies

## Purpose

This meta-policy establishes the framework for developing, approving, maintaining, and retiring organizational policies to ensure governance consistency and effectiveness.

## Scope

This policy applies to all organizational policies, standards, guidelines, and procedures.

## Policy Hierarchy

| Level | Description | Approval |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Policy** | Mandatory requirements | Board/Executive |
| **Standard** | Specific implementation requirements | Function Head |
| **Guideline** | Recommended practices | Function Lead |
| **Procedure** | Step-by-step instructions | Process Owner |

## Policy Lifecycle

### Development

1. Identify policy need (regulatory, operational, strategic)
2. Assign policy owner
3. Draft policy following template
4. Conduct stakeholder review
5. Legal/compliance review
6. Submit for approval

### Approval

| Policy Scope | Approval Authority |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Enterprise-wide | Board or Executive Committee |
| Business unit | BU Head |
| Function-specific | Function Head |

### Communication

- Publish in policy repository
- Notify affected stakeholders
- Conduct training if required
- Update related procedures

### Review

- Annual review for all policies
- Ad hoc review for regulatory changes
- Version control maintained

### Retirement

- Assess continued relevance
- Obtain retirement approval
- Archive with retention date
- Communicate sunset

## Policy Components

Required elements for all policies:

- Purpose and scope
- Policy statements
- Roles and responsibilities
- Compliance and enforcement
- Exceptions process
- Related documents
- Version history

## Exceptions

- Document exception request
- Obtain approval from policy owner
- Time-limit all exceptions
- Track and review periodically

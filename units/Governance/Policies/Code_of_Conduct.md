# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This Code of Conduct establishes the ethical standards and behavioral expectations for all employees, contractors, officers, and directors of the organization.

## Core Values

### Integrity

Act honestly and ethically in all business dealings. Never misrepresent facts, falsify records, or engage in deceptive practices.

### Respect

Treat all individuals with dignity and respect. Foster an inclusive environment free from discrimination, harassment, and bullying.

### Accountability

Take responsibility for your actions and decisions. Report concerns promptly and cooperate fully with investigations.

### Excellence

Strive for high-quality work and continuous improvement. Meet commitments and deliver on promises.

## Standards of Conduct

### Workplace Behavior

- Maintain a professional, respectful work environment
- Communicate openly and constructively
- Support diversity, equity, and inclusion
- Avoid conflicts of interest or disclose them promptly

### Business Practices

- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- Protect confidential and proprietary information
- Use company resources responsibly
- Maintain accurate records and reports

### External Relationships

- Treat customers, vendors, and partners fairly
- Never offer or accept improper payments or gifts
- Compete ethically and lawfully
- Represent the organization accurately

## Reporting Concerns

Employees are encouraged to report violations through:

- Direct supervisor or manager
- Human Resources
- Compliance Officer
- Anonymous ethics hotline

See [Whistleblower Policy](./Whistleblower_Policy.md) for protection against retaliation.

## Consequences

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination, as well as potential legal action.

# Governance KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Corporate governance performance indicators measuring board effectiveness, policy adherence, and stakeholder accountability.

## Governance KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Board Meeting Attendance | 90%+ | Per meeting | Corp Secretary |
| Committee Charter Compliance | 100% | Annual | Corporate Gov |
| Policy Review Completion | 100% annual | Annual | Policy Mgr |
| Shareholder Resolution Response | 100% timely | Per resolution | Corp Secretary |
| Director Independence | Per requirements | Annual | Nom/Gov Committee |
| ESG Score | Top quartile | Annual | Sustainability |
| Governance Rating (ISS/Glass Lewis) | Positive | Annual | IR/Governance |

## Board Effectiveness

### Board Composition

| Dimension | Target | Tracking |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Independence | >majority | Annual |
| Diversity | Per policy | Annual |
| Skills matrix coverage | 100% | Annual |
| Tenure balance | Mixed | Annual |
| Overboarding compliance | 100% | Annual |

### Board Operations

- Meeting frequency: Per charter
- Materials distributed: 5+ days prior
- Executive session held: Each meeting
- Board evaluation: Annual

## Committee Performance

### Audit Committee

- Financial expert present: Yes
- Meetings per year: 4+
- Private auditor sessions: Quarterly
- Whistleblower oversight: Active

### Compensation Committee

- Independent composition: 100%
- Say-on-pay approval: 70%+
- Pay-for-performance alignment
- Peer group review: Annual

### Nominating/Governance Committee

- Director search process: Documented
- Succession planning: Reviewed annually
- Governance guidelines: Current

## Policy Governance

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Policies reviewed on schedule | 100% | Annual |
| Policy exceptions granted | Documented | Ongoing |
| Policy violations | Investigated | Per incident |
| Employee attestation | 100% | Annual |

## Stakeholder Engagement

### Shareholder Engagement

- Engagement meetings held
- Proxy advisory firm engagement
- ESG investor meetings
- Shareholder proposal response

# Governance Unit - Technical Documentation

## Overview

Establishes frameworks for decision-making, authority distribution, and policy enforcement.

The Governance unit establishes and maintains the organizational governance framework, ensuring accountability, transparency, and ethical conduct across all operations. It oversees policy development, compliance monitoring, and stakeholder governance.
## Directory Structure

```text
Governance/
├── README.md        # Unit overview and governance framework
├── MeetingNotes/    # Governance committee meeting records
├── Policies/        # Governance policies and charters
├── Processes/       # Governance review procedures
└── Reports/         # Governance assessments and audits
```text

## Document Types

### Governance Policies

Corporate governance frameworks, organizational charters, decision-making authority matrices, conflict of interest policies, transparency standards, and stakeholder engagement policies.

### Governance Processes

Policy development lifecycle, periodic governance review cycles, compliance monitoring procedures, governance risk assessment workflows, and stakeholder communication protocols.

### Reports

Governance effectiveness assessments, policy compliance dashboards, governance risk reports, framework maturity evaluations, and annual governance reviews.

# Risk Advisory Board KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Risk advisory board performance indicators measuring risk oversight quality, emerging risk identification, and risk strategy support.

## RAB KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Risk Appetite Input | Incorporated | Annual | CRO |
| Emerging Risk Identification | Early warning | Quarterly | RAB Chair |
| Risk Assessment Review | High quality | Per assessment | RAB Liaison |
| Crisis Preparedness Input | Tested and improved | Annual | CRO |
| Industry Risk Benchmarking | Insights shared | Quarterly | RAB Liaison |
| Risk Culture Assessment | Input provided | Annual | CRO |
| Regulatory Risk Guidance | Timely and expert | As needed | RAB Liaison |

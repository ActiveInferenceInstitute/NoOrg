# Product Management Unit

## Overview

The Product Management unit drives strategic product direction, development, launch, and lifecycle management. We bridge technical and business worlds, collaborating with engineering, marketing, sales, and support to deliver products that fulfill our vision and meet market needs.

This unit defines the *what* and *why* for products, ensuring alignment with customer needs and business objectives.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Product Strategy & Vision**: Define and communicate product vision aligned with company goals
- **Roadmap Planning**: Develop and maintain product roadmap, prioritizing based on market analysis, customer feedback, and business value
- **Market & Customer Research**: Understand market trends, competitive landscape, and customer needs
- **Product Discovery & Definition**: Translate strategy into requirements, user stories, and specifications
- **Lifecycle Management**: Manage products from conception through end-of-life
- **Go-to-Market Strategy**: Partner with marketing and sales on product launches
- **Cross-Functional Collaboration**: Work with engineering, design, marketing, sales, and support
- **Performance Monitoring**: Track product metrics and iterate based on data and feedback

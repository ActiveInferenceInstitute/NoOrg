# Intelligence KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Business/competitive intelligence performance indicators measuring market insight quality, decision support, and strategic advantage.

## Intelligence KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Intelligence Request Fulfillment | 95%+ within SLA | Monthly | Intel Director |
| Strategic Decision Support | Used in major decisions | Quarterly | Intel Director |
| Competitive Win/Loss Insight | 100% key deals | Per deal | Win/Loss Analyst |
| Market Signal Identification | Early detection rate | Monthly | Market Intel |
| Stakeholder Satisfaction | 90%+ | Quarterly | Intel Director |
| Report Accuracy | 99%+ verified | Per report | Analyst Lead |
| Source Coverage | Comprehensive | Quarterly | Research Lead |

## Competitive Intelligence

### Competitor Monitoring

| Competitor Tier | Monitoring Depth | Update Frequency |
|-----------------|------------------|------------------|
| Tier 1 (primary) | Full profile | Weekly |
| Tier 2 (secondary) | Key signals | Monthly |
| Tier 3 (emerging) | Watch list | Quarterly |

- Win rate vs. each competitor
- Loss reason categorization
- Battlecard effectiveness
- Sales enablement impact

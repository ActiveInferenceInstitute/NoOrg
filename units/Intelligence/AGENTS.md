# Intelligence Unit - Technical Documentation

## Overview

Gathers and analyzes market intelligence, competitive landscapes, and strategic insights.

The Intelligence unit provides strategic intelligence capabilities including market analysis, competitive intelligence, trend forecasting, and data-driven insights. It transforms raw data into actionable intelligence for organizational decision-making.
## Directory Structure

```text
Intelligence/
├── README.md        # Unit overview and intelligence framework
├── MeetingNotes/    # Intelligence briefing records
├── Policies/        # Intelligence governance and ethics policies
├── Processes/       # Intelligence cycle procedures
└── Reports/         # Intelligence assessments and analyses
```text

## Document Types

### Intelligence Policies

Data collection ethics, analysis standards, dissemination protocols, source protection policies, and intelligence sharing agreements.

### Intelligence Processes

Intelligence cycle procedures (planning, collection, processing, analysis, dissemination), OSINT methodologies, competitive analysis frameworks, and trend detection workflows.

### Reports

Strategic intelligence assessments, market analysis reports, competitive landscape maps, emerging trend reports, and early warning indicators.

# Legal Communication Plan

## Overview

Stakeholder communication strategy for the Legal Unit.

## Stakeholder Matrix

| Stakeholder | Interest Level | Influence | Communication Approach |
|-------------|---------------|-----------|------------------------|
| Executive Leadership | High | High | Regular briefings |
| Peer Units | Medium | Medium | Collaboration forums |
| External Partners | Varies | Varies | Formal communications |

## Communication Channels

| Channel | Purpose | Frequency |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Email | Updates and announcements | As needed |
| Meetings | Discussion and decisions | Scheduled |
| Reports | Status and metrics | Periodic |
| Dashboard | Real-time visibility | Continuous |

## Key Messages

1. Unit mission and value
2. Current priorities
3. Success stories
4. Resource needs

## Operational Metrics

### Contract Management

- Contracts processed per month
- Average negotiation cycles
- Self-service contract adoption rate
- Renewal tracking compliance

### Litigation Management

- Open matters by category
- Reserve accuracy (actual vs. reserved)
- Case age and disposition time
- Discovery cost per matter

### Intellectual Property

- Patents filed/granted
- Trademark registrations
- IP portfolio value
- Infringement actions

### Compliance

- Policy acknowledgment rate: 100%
- Training completion rate: 100%
- Regulatory filing on-time rate: 100%

## Risk Metrics

| Legal Risk | Metric | Threshold |

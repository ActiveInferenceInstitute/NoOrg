## Logo Usage

### Clear Space
- Minimum clear space = x height of logo
- No elements within clear zone
- Consistent positioning

### Minimum Size
| Application | Minimum |
|-------------|---------|
| Print | 1 inch width |
| Digital | 120 pixels |
| Favicon | 16x16 pixels |

### Prohibited Uses
- Distortion or rotation
- Unapproved colors
- Low resolution
- Busy backgrounds
- Modification

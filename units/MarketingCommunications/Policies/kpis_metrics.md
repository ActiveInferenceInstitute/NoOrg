# Marketing Communications KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Integrated marketing communications performance indicators measuring campaign effectiveness, brand consistency, and channel performance.

## MarComm KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Campaign ROI | 3x+ | Per campaign | MarComm Director |
| Brand Consistency Score | 95%+ | Quarterly | Brand Manager |
| Content Engagement Rate | Industry benchmark+ | Monthly | Content Lead |
| Integrated Campaign Reach | Per plan | Per campaign | Media Lead |
| Creative Approval Cycle | <5 days | Weekly | Creative Dir |
| Agency Performance Score | 80%+ | Quarterly | Agency Lead |
| Message Recall | 60%+ | Per campaign | Brand Manager |

## Campaign Metrics

### Campaign Performance

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Reach | Per plan | Per campaign |
| Frequency | Optimal range | Per campaign |
| Engagement | Benchmark+ | Per campaign |
| Conversion | Per goal | Per campaign |

### Attribution

- Multi-touch attribution
- View-through conversions
- Assisted conversions
- Cross-channel impact

## Brand Metrics

### Brand Health

- Awareness (aided/unaided)
- Consideration
- Preference
- Loyalty/advocacy

### Brand Consistency

- Visual identity compliance: 100%
- Message consistency: 95%+
- Tone of voice adherence
- Partner/channel compliance

## Content Performance

### Content Types

| Content | Key Metrics | Target |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Blog posts | Views, shares, time | Engagement |
| Videos | Views, completion, shares | 50%+ completion |
| Whitepapers | Downloads, leads | Lead quality |
| Social posts | Engagement rate | 3%+ |

### Content Quality

- Content calendar adherence
- Repurposing efficiency
- SEO performance
- Localization quality

## Channel Metrics

### Paid Media

- CPM/CPC/CPA efficiency
- ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)
- Quality score
- Share of voice

### Owned Media

- Website traffic
- Email performance
- Social following growth
- Community engagement

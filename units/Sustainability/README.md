# Sustainability Unit

## Overview

The Sustainability Unit champions environmental, social, and governance (ESG) excellence across the organization.

## Mission

To integrate sustainable practices into all organizational activities and demonstrate leadership in corporate responsibility.

## Key Responsibilities

- Environmental impact reduction
- ESG strategy and reporting
- Stakeholder engagement on sustainability
- Carbon footprint management
- Sustainable supply chain practices

# Sustainability KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

ESG performance indicators measuring environmental impact, social responsibility, and governance excellence.

## Sustainability KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Carbon Emissions (Scope 1+2) | Net Zero by [year] | Annual | Sustainability Dir |
| Renewable Energy % | 100% by [year] | Quarterly | Energy Mgr |
| Waste Diversion Rate | 90%+ | Monthly | Facilities |
| Water Intensity | Year-over-year reduction | Quarterly | Sustainability |
| ESG Rating (MSCI, etc.) | Top quartile | Annual | Sustainability Dir |
| Diversity Representation | Per published goals | Quarterly | HR/DEI |
| Community Investment | % of profit | Annual | CSR |

## Environmental Metrics

### Carbon Footprint

| Scope | Metric | Target |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Scope 1 | Direct emissions | Net zero path |
| Scope 2 | Energy indirect | 100% renewable |
| Scope 3 | Value chain | Reduction targets |

### Resource Efficiency

- Energy intensity (kWh/revenue)
- Water intensity (gal/revenue)
- Waste generated per employee
- Packaging sustainability %

### Climate Resilience

- Climate risk assessment: Complete
- Adaptation plans: In place
- TCFD reporting: Compliant
- Science-based targets: Validated

## Social Metrics

### Workforce

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Gender diversity (leadership) | 40%+ | Quarterly |
| Ethnic diversity (US) | Per market | Quarterly |
| Pay equity ratio | 1.0 | Annual |
| Safety incident rate | Zero harm | Monthly |

### Community Impact

- Volunteer hours contributed
- Charitable giving ($ and % of profit)
- Local economic impact
- Education/skills programs

### Supply Chain Responsibility

- Supplier ESG assessments: 100% critical
- Conflict minerals compliance: 100%
- Modern slavery due diligence: Complete
- Sustainable sourcing %

## Governance Metrics

### ESG Governance

- Board ESG oversight: Active
- ESG-linked executive compensation: Yes
- Stakeholder engagement: Regular
- Transparency/disclosure: SASB/GRI

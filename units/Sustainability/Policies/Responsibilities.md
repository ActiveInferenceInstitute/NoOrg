# Sustainability Responsibilities

## Overview

Defines the core responsibilities of the Sustainability Unit.

## Primary Responsibilities

1. **Strategic Planning**: Define and execute Sustainability strategy
2. **Operations**: Manage day-to-day Sustainability activities
3. **Stakeholder Management**: Coordinate with internal and external stakeholders
4. **Compliance**: Ensure adherence to policies and regulations
5. **Reporting**: Provide regular status and metrics reporting

# Executive Unit Governance

## Overview

Governance framework for executive leadership operations and decision-making.

## Executive Committee Structure

| Role | Responsibility | Meeting Participation |
|------|----------------|----------------------|
| CEO | Overall strategy, board relations | All meetings |
| CFO | Financial strategy, investor relations | All meetings |
| COO | Operations, execution | All meetings |
| CTO/CIO | Technology strategy | All meetings |
| CHRO | People strategy, culture | All meetings |
| CLO/GC | Legal, risk, compliance | All meetings |
| CMO | Marketing, brand | Monthly+ |
| CSO | Strategy, M&A | Quarterly+ |

## Decision Authority Matrix

| Decision Type | Authority | Approval Level | Board Notification |
|---------------|-----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Strategic Initiatives | CEO | Board approval | Yes |
| Annual Budget | CFO | Board approval | Yes |
| Capital Expenditure >$1M | CFO | CEO + Board | Yes |
| M&A Transactions | CEO | Board approval | Yes |
| Executive Hiring | CHRO | CEO approval | FYI |
| Policy Changes (material) | CLO | CEO + Board | Yes |
| Operational Changes | COO | CEO approval | No |
| Technology Investments >$500K | CTO | CFO + CEO | FYI |

## Meeting Cadence

| Meeting | Frequency | Duration | Chair | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|----------|-------|---------|
| Executive Committee | Weekly | 2 hours | CEO | Operational alignment |
| Strategic Planning | Monthly | 4 hours | CSO | Strategy review |
| Board Preparation | Quarterly | 2 hours | CEO | Board meeting prep |
| Town Hall | Quarterly | 1 hour | CEO | All-hands communication |
| Off-site Strategy | Annual | 2 days | CEO | Long-term planning |

## Escalation Framework

### Level 1: Business Unit

- Routine operational issues
- Budget variances <5%
- Standard risk items

### Level 2: Executive Owner

- Cross-functional issues
- Budget variances 5-15%
- Emerging risks

### Level 3: CEO / Executive Committee

- Material business impact
- Budget variances >15%
- Strategic risks

### Level 4: Board of Directors

- Corporate-level decisions
- M&A, major capital
- Crisis situations

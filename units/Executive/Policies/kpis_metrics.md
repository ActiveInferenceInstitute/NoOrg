# Executive KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Executive leadership performance indicators measuring organizational health, strategic execution, and stakeholder value.

## Executive KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Revenue Growth | Per plan | Quarterly | CEO |
| EBITDA Margin | Per plan | Quarterly | CFO |
| Total Shareholder Return | Outperform peers | Annual | CEO |
| Employee Engagement | 80%+ | Annual | CHRO |
| Customer NPS | 50+ | Quarterly | CCO |
| Strategic Initiative Delivery | 90%+ | Quarterly | COO |
| ESG Score | Top quartile | Annual | CEO |

## Organizational Health Metrics

### Financial Health

- Revenue vs. plan
- Profitability vs. plan
- Cash flow generation
- Balance sheet strength

### Operational Health

- Productivity metrics by function
- Quality indicators
- Customer satisfaction trends
- Innovation pipeline value

### People Health

- Engagement score
- Regrettable turnover
- Diversity metrics
- Leadership bench strength

## Board Reporting Metrics

| Category | Key Metrics | Reporting Cadence |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Financial | Revenue, margins, cash | Quarterly |
| Strategic | Initiative status, market position | Quarterly |
| Risk | Top enterprise risks, mitigation | Quarterly |
| Compliance | Material issues, audit status | Quarterly |
| Talent | Succession, engagement, DEI | Semi-annual |

## Executive Dashboard

| View | Content | Audience |
|------|---------|----------|
| CEO Dashboard | Enterprise KPIs, alerts | CEO |
| CFO Dashboard | Financial metrics, forecasts | CFO/Finance |
| COO Dashboard | Operations, delivery | COO/Operations |

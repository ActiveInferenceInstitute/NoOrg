# Executive Compliance Requirements

## Overview

Compliance obligations and controls for the Executive Unit.

## Regulatory Requirements

| Requirement | Description | Owner |
|-------------|-------------|-------|
| Internal Policies | Adherence to organizational policies | All |
| Data Protection | Privacy and data handling | Specialist |
| Documentation | Record keeping and audit trails | Manager |

## Control Framework

### Preventive Controls
- Policy documentation and training
- Access controls and segregation of duties
- Approval workflows

### Detective Controls
- Regular audits and reviews
- Monitoring and reporting
- Exception tracking

### Corrective Controls
- Issue remediation process
- Root cause analysis
- Continuous improvement

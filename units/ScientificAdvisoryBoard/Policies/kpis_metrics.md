# Scientific Advisory Board KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Scientific advisory board performance indicators measuring research guidance quality, scientific rigor, and innovation support.

## SAB KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Research Direction Input | Adopted | Quarterly | Research Director |
| Scientific Review Quality | Rigorous | Per review | SAB Liaison |
| Publication Strategy Guidance | Provided | Quarterly | Research Lead |
| Grant Application Support | Available | As needed | SAB Chair |
| Clinical/Regulatory Input | Expert level | Per submission | SAB Liaison |
| Scientific Collaboration Facilitation | Connections made | Quarterly | Research Director |
| IP Strategy Input | Strategic value | Quarterly | IP Lead |

## Scientific Guidance Metrics

### Research Program Input

| Area | Metric | Tracking |
|------|--------|----------|
| Research priorities | Recommendations adopted | Annually |
| Methodology review | Quality of feedback | Per review |
| Data interpretation | Expert input | Per study |
| Publication review | Journals and positioning | Per manuscript |

### Scientific Rigor

- Protocol review quality
- Statistical methodology guidance
- Reproducibility standards input
- Peer review simulation effectiveness

## Advisory Sessions

### Meeting Effectiveness

- Scientific depth of discussions
- Cross-disciplinary insights
- Constructive challenge rate
- Career development advice for researchers

### Focus Areas

| Focus Area | Review Frequency | Lead Advisor |
|------------|------------------|--------------|
| Core research | Quarterly | [Advisor] |
| Clinical translation | As applicable | [Advisor] |
| Regulatory science | As needed | [Advisor] |
| Ethics and integrity | Annually | [Advisor] |
| Emerging science | Semi-annual | [Advisor] |

## Advisor Expertise

### Expertise Coverage

- Basic research: Covered
- Translational research: Covered
- Clinical research: Covered
- Regulatory pathway: Covered
- Bioethics: Covered
- Key therapeutic areas: Covered

### Academic Network

- University collaborations facilitated
- Visiting scientist arrangements
- Joint research proposals
- Conference introductions

## Impact Metrics

### Research Advancement

- Publications influenced by SAB input
- Grant success with SAB support
- IP filings with SAB contribution
- Regulatory submissions guided

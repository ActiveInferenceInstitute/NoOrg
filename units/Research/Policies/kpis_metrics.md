# Research KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Research performance indicators measuring discovery impact, publication output, and knowledge advancement.

## Research KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Peer-Reviewed Publications | Per annual target | Quarterly | Research Director |
| Citation Impact Factor | Growing | Annual | Research Director |
| Grant Funding Secured | Per target | Annual | Grants Manager |
| Research Partnerships | Active engagements | Quarterly | Partnerships Lead |
| Patent Applications | Per target | Quarterly | IP Lead |
| Technology Transfer | License agreements | Annual | Tech Transfer |
| Research Integrity Compliance | 100% | Annual | Compliance |

## Publication Metrics

### Output Quality

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|

## Funding Metrics

### Grant Success

- Funding success rate: Target %

### Financial Management

- Grant budget adherence: 100%
- Indirect cost recovery
- Effort certification accuracy
- Carryover management

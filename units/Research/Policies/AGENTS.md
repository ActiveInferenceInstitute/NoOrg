# Research Unit Policies - Technical Documentation

## Overview

This directory contains the core policies governing the Research Unit, ensuring scientific integrity, methodological rigor, and compliance with ethical standards.

## Directory Structure

- **ResearchMethodology.md**: Standards for research methods.
- **EthicalGuidelines.md**: Ethics compliance framework.
- **DataHandling.md**: Policies for data management and security.
- **PublicationPolicy.md**: Rules regarding external publication.

## Key Policies

### ResearchMethodology.md

Defines the approved methodologies and verification steps for all research output.

### EthicalGuidelines.md

Ensures all research activities adhere to the organization's ethical standards.

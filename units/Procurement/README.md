# Procurement Unit

## Overview

The Procurement Unit manages the acquisition of goods and services, ensuring value for money, compliance, and strong vendor relationships.

## Mission

To deliver strategic sourcing and procurement services that optimize costs while maintaining quality and compliance.

## Key Responsibilities

- Strategic sourcing and vendor selection
- Contract negotiation and management
- Purchase order processing
- Vendor performance management
- Spend analytics and optimization

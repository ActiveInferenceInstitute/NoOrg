# Procurement Positions & Personas

## Overview

Defines the key roles and personas within the Procurement Unit.

## Positions

| Position | Level | Key Responsibilities |
|----------|-------|---------------------|
| Procurement Director | Senior | Unit leadership and strategy |
| Procurement Manager | Mid | Operations and team management |
| Procurement Specialist | Individual | Core functional execution |
| Procurement Analyst | Entry | Analysis and support |

## Personas

### Strategic Leader
Focused on long-term planning and stakeholder management.

### Operational Manager
Focused on day-to-day execution and team coordination.

### Domain Expert
Deep technical/functional expertise in Procurement domain.

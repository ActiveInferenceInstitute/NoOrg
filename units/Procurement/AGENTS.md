# Procurement Unit - Technical Documentation

## Overview

Manages sourcing, purchasing, vendor management, and supply chain relationships to ensure cost-effective and reliable acquisition of goods and services.

## Directory Structure

```text
Procurement/
├── README.md        # Unit overview
├── Charter.md       # Unit charter
├── MeetingNotes/    # Procurement meeting records
├── Policies/        # Procurement policies
├── Processes/       # Procurement procedures
└── Reports/         # Procurement metrics and reports
```

## Key Components

### Strategic Sourcing

Vendor selection, RFP management, and contract negotiation.

### Vendor Management

Supplier relationship management and performance monitoring.

### Purchase Operations

Order processing, invoice management, and spend analysis.

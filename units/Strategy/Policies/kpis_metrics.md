# Strategy KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Strategic planning performance indicators measuring organizational direction, initiative execution, and market positioning.

## Strategy KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Strategic Initiative Completion | 85%+ on time | Quarterly | CSO |
| Market Share Growth | Per annual target | Quarterly | Strategy |
| Revenue from New Markets | 20%+ | Annual | CSO |
| Strategic Plan Refresh | Annual cycle | Annual | CSO |
| Competitive Win Rate | Trending up | Quarterly | Intelligence |
| M&A Pipeline Value | Per target | Quarterly | Corp Dev |
| Strategic NPS (Board/Exec) | 80%+ | Annual | CSO |

## Strategic Initiative Metrics

### Portfolio Health

- Initiatives on track: 80%+
- Initiatives at risk: <15%
- Initiatives off track: <5%
- Resource allocation vs. strategic priority alignment

### Execution Quality

- Milestone achievement rate
- Budget adherence
- Scope stability
- Stakeholder satisfaction

## Market Analysis Metrics

| Dimension | Metric | Tracking |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Market Size | TAM/SAM/SOM | Annual |
| Market Growth | YoY growth rate | Quarterly |
| Market Share | Company share % | Quarterly |
| Competitive Position | Ranking vs. peers | Quarterly |

## Planning Cycle Performance

### Annual Planning

- Plan completion on schedule
- Stakeholder input collected
- Board approval timeline
- Communication cascade completion

### Quarterly Business Reviews

- QBR deck quality score
- Action item closure rate
- Forecast accuracy

## M&A and Corporate Development

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Deal pipeline coverage | 3x target | Monthly |
| Due diligence cycle time | <90 days | Per deal |
| Integration success rate | 95%+ | Post-close |
| Synergy realization | 100% of target | 24 months |

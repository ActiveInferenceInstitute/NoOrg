# Competitive Intelligence Ethics Policy

## Purpose

This policy establishes ethical boundaries and acceptable practices for gathering, analyzing, and using competitive intelligence while maintaining legal compliance and organizational integrity.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties engaged in competitive intelligence activities on behalf of the organization.

## Ethical Principles

### Do's (Acceptable Practices)

- Analyze publicly available information (websites, press releases, SEC filings)
- Attend industry conferences and trade shows
- Review published analyst reports and market research
- Conduct customer surveys and win/loss interviews (with disclosure)
- Monitor social media and public forums
- Gather information at industry events through normal networking

### Don'ts (Prohibited Practices)

- Misrepresent identity to obtain information
- Seek proprietary information through deception
- Hire competitors' employees primarily for their confidential knowledge
- Encourage employees to violate confidentiality agreements
- Access competitor systems without authorization
- Engage in bribery or improper inducements
- Use stolen or misappropriated information

## Information Sources

| Source Category | Acceptable | Caution Required |
|-----------------|------------|------------------|
| Public filings | Yes | - |
| Industry publications | Yes | - |
| Conferences/events | Yes | Ensure proper disclosure |
| Former employees | Limited | Must respect prior NDAs |
| Vendors/suppliers | Limited | No deception |
| Customers | Yes | With disclosure |

## Handling Confidential Information

If confidential competitor information is inadvertently received:

1. Stop reviewing immediately
2. Notify Legal Department
3. Follow Legal guidance on disposition
4. Document the incident

## Compliance

- All CI activities must comply with applicable laws
- Anti-competitive behavior is strictly prohibited
- Document information sources for audit purposes
- Report concerns per [Whistleblower Policy](../../Governance/Policies/Whistleblower_Policy.md)

# Confidentiality Policy - Strategy

## Purpose

This policy establishes confidentiality requirements for strategic information to protect competitive advantage and ensure appropriate handling of sensitive business intelligence.

## Scope

This policy applies to all strategic information including plans, analyses, competitive intelligence, M&A activities, and other strategy-related materials.

## Classification Levels

| Level | Examples | Handling |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Highly Confidential** | M&A plans, strategic pivots, major investments | Need-to-know, encrypted storage |
| **Confidential** | Strategic plans, competitive analyses | Authorized personnel only |
| **Internal** | Market trends, industry analyses | All employees |
| **Public** | Published strategy statements | No restrictions |

## Protection Requirements

### Highly Confidential

- Access limited to named individuals
- Encrypted storage and transmission
- No copies without approval
- Audit trail maintained
- Physical security for printed materials

### Confidential

- Access controlled by role
- Secure storage (password protected)
- No external sharing without approval
- Watermarking for sensitive documents

## Handling Guidelines

### Meetings

- Confirm attendee authorization before sharing
- Use secure conference tools
- Clear screens when leaving
- Secure notes and materials

### Documents

- Apply appropriate classification labels
- Store in designated secure locations
- Shred physical copies when no longer needed
- Use secure channels for transmission

### Third Parties

- NDA required before sharing confidential information
- Limit to minimum necessary information
- Track what was shared with whom
- Written authorization from Strategy Director

## Breach Protocol

1. Report immediately to Strategy Director and Legal
2. Assess scope of potential exposure
3. Implement containment measures
4. Conduct investigation
5. Take remedial action

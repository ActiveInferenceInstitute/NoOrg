# Strategic Planning Policy

## Purpose

This policy establishes the framework, principles, and governance for the organization's strategic planning activities to ensure alignment, accountability, and effective resource allocation.

## Scope

This policy applies to all strategic planning activities across the organization, including corporate strategy, business unit planning, and functional strategies.

## Policy Statements

### Planning Horizon

- **Long-term strategy**: 3-5 year outlook, reviewed annually
- **Medium-term plan**: 1-3 year rolling plan, updated annually
- **Annual operating plan**: Detailed 12-month plan with quarterly reviews

### Planning Principles

1. **Customer-centric**: Strategy must address customer needs and market dynamics
2. **Data-driven**: Decisions grounded in market research and performance data
3. **Aligned**: All plans cascade from corporate strategy
4. **Resource-aware**: Plans must be realistic given resource constraints
5. **Adaptable**: Built-in flexibility for market changes

### Strategic Priorities

- Maximum of 5-7 strategic priorities at any time
- Each priority has an executive sponsor
- Priorities reviewed quarterly by Executive Committee

## Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| CEO | Final approval of corporate strategy |
| Strategy Unit | Facilitate planning process, synthesize inputs |
| Business Unit Leaders | Develop unit plans aligned to corporate strategy |
| Finance | Financial modeling, resource allocation |
| Board of Directors | Approve major strategic decisions |

## Governance

- Annual strategic planning cycle per [Strategic Planning Cycle](../Processes/Strategic_Planning_Cycle.md)
- Quarterly strategy reviews with Executive Committee
- Semi-annual Board strategy sessions
- Monthly strategic initiative tracking

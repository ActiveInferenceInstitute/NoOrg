# Market Analysis Policy

## Purpose

This policy establishes standards for conducting market analysis to support strategic decision-making and ensure consistent, high-quality market intelligence.

## Scope

This policy applies to all market analysis activities including market sizing, segmentation, trend analysis, and opportunity assessment.

## Analysis Standards

### Required Elements

All market analyses must include:

- Clear methodology description
- Data sources and timeframes
- Assumptions and limitations
- Confidence levels for projections
- Actionable recommendations

### Quality Criteria

| Dimension | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Accuracy | Multiple source triangulation |
| Currency | Data < 12 months old unless historical |
| Relevance | Aligned to strategic questions |
| Objectivity | Unbiased methodology |

## Types of Market Analysis

### Standard Analyses

- **Market sizing**: TAM/SAM/SOM estimation
- **Segmentation**: Customer and market segment definition
- **Trend analysis**: Market dynamics and forecasting
- **Competitive landscape**: Market share and positioning

### Ad Hoc Analyses

- M&A target market assessment
- New market entry evaluation
- Product opportunity sizing
- Regulatory impact analysis

## Data Management

- Primary research data stored in approved repositories
- Third-party data managed per licensing agreements
- Proprietary analyses classified appropriately
- Retention per records management policy

## Approval and Distribution

| Analysis Type | Approval Required | Distribution |
|---------------|-------------------|--------------|
| Standard recurring | Strategy Lead | Per schedule |
| Executive briefing | Strategy Director | Executive Team |
| Board materials | Chief Strategy Officer | Board only |
| External use | Legal + CSO | Controlled |

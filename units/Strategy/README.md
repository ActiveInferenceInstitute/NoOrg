# Strategy Unit

The Strategy unit drives enterprise-wide strategic planning, competitive positioning, and long-term value creation.

## Purpose

Develop and steward the organization's strategic direction, ensuring alignment across all business units and enabling informed decision-making through market intelligence and scenario planning.

## Core Functions

- **Strategic Planning**: Annual and multi-year strategic planning cycles
- **Market Intelligence**: Competitive analysis and market trend monitoring
- **Portfolio Management**: Strategic initiative prioritization and resource allocation
- **M&A Strategy**: Acquisition and partnership opportunity evaluation
- **Scenario Planning**: Future-state modeling and strategy stress-testing

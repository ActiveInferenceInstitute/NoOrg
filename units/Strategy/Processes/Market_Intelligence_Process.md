# Market Intelligence Process

## Purpose

This process establishes how market intelligence is gathered, analyzed, and distributed to support strategic and operational decision-making.

## Scope

Market intelligence includes:

- Market size and growth analysis
- Industry trend monitoring
- Customer insights
- Regulatory and macroeconomic developments
- Technology and innovation tracking

## Process Steps

### Step 1: Intelligence Requirements

#### Stakeholder Needs Assessment

- Conduct annual requirements gathering
- Maintain standing intelligence priorities
- Accept ad hoc requests through designated channel

#### Priority Categories

| Priority | Response Time | Depth |
|----------|---------------|-------|
| Critical | 24-48 hours | Rapid assessment |
| High | 1 week | Thorough analysis |
| Standard | 2-4 weeks | Comprehensive |
| Background | Ongoing | Monitoring |

### Step 2: Collection

#### Primary Research

- Customer interviews and surveys
- Industry expert consultations
- Trade show attendance
- Partner and channel feedback

#### Secondary Research

- Industry publications and reports
- Government and regulatory sources
- Academic research
- News and media monitoring
- Social media and forum analysis

### Step 3: Analysis

#### Standard Analyses

| Analysis Type | Frequency | Output |
|---------------|-----------|--------|
| Market sizing | Annual | TAM/SAM/SOM report |
| Trend analysis | Quarterly | Trend brief |
| Segment analysis | Semi-annual | Segment profiles |
| Regulatory scan | Monthly | Regulatory digest |

#### Analytical Methods

- Quantitative market modeling
- Qualitative trend synthesis
- Expert judgment calibration
- Scenario integration

### Step 4: Distribution

#### Delivery Channels

- Intelligence portal (self-service)
- Scheduled reports and newsletters
- Executive briefings
- Strategy session inputs

### Step 5: Feedback

- Track intelligence utilization
- Gather stakeholder feedback
- Continuously improve processes

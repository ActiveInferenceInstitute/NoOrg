# Competitor Analysis Process

## Purpose

This process defines how to systematically analyze competitors to inform strategic decision-making while adhering to ethical standards.

## Process Steps

### Step 1: Competitor Identification

#### Competitor Categories

| Category | Definition | Analysis Depth |
|----------|------------|----------------|
| Primary | Direct competitors for target customers | Deep analysis |
| Secondary | Partial overlap in market/offering | Regular monitoring |
| Emerging | Potential future competitors | Watch list |
| Substitutes | Alternative solutions | Trend monitoring |

### Step 2: Information Gathering

#### Ethical Sources

- Public filings (10-K, 10-Q, proxy statements)
- Company websites and press releases
- Industry analyst reports
- Trade publications and news
- Patent and trademark databases
- Job postings
- Conference presentations
- Customer interviews (with disclosure)

See [Competitive Intelligence Ethics Policy](../Policies/Competitive_Intelligence_Ethics_Policy.md) for guidance.

### Step 3: Analysis Framework

#### Competitor Profile Elements

- Company overview and financials
- Product/service portfolio
- Target markets and positioning
- Pricing and business model
- Go-to-market strategy
- Strengths and weaknesses
- Strategic direction

#### Analytical Tools

| Tool | Application |
|------|-------------|
| SWOT | Strength/weakness assessment |
| Porter's Five Forces | Industry dynamics |
| Value chain analysis | Competitive advantage sources |
| Positioning maps | Market positioning |

### Step 4: Synthesis and Reporting

#### Standard Deliverables

- Competitor profiles (updated quarterly)
- Competitive landscape overview
- Win/loss analysis synthesis
- Strategic implications

#### Distribution

- Competitor alerts: As needed
- Quarterly updates: Leadership team
- Deep-dive analyses: Strategy reviews

### Step 5: Integration

- Feed into strategic planning cycle
- Inform product roadmap discussions
- Support sales battlecard updates
- Guide M&A opportunity assessment

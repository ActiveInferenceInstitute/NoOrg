# Strategic Planning Cycle Process

## Purpose

This process defines the annual strategic planning cycle including timeline, responsibilities, deliverables, and governance.

## Annual Planning Calendar

| Phase | Timeline | Activities | Lead |
|-------|----------|------------|------|
| **Environmental Scan** | Q3 (July-Aug) | Market analysis, competitive review, trend assessment | Strategy |
| **Strategic Review** | Q3 (Aug-Sep) | Mission/vision validation, strategic pillar review | Executive Team |
| **Planning Sessions** | Q3-Q4 (Sep-Oct) | Business unit planning, initiative development | BU Leaders |
| **Integration** | Q4 (Nov) | Cross-functional alignment, resource allocation | Strategy/Finance |
| **Board Approval** | Q4 (Dec) | Board presentation and approval | CEO/CFO |
| **Cascade** | Q1 (Jan) | Communication, goal setting, execution launch | All Leaders |

## Process Steps

### Phase 1: Environmental Scanning

1. Conduct market and industry analysis
2. Complete competitive landscape assessment
3. Gather customer and stakeholder insights
4. Identify macroeconomic and regulatory trends
5. Synthesize findings into strategic context report

### Phase 2: Strategic Review

1. Review mission, vision, and values for relevance
2. Assess current strategy performance
3. Identify strategic gaps and opportunities
4. Validate or refine strategic pillars
5. Define strategic priorities for planning horizon

### Phase 3: Business Unit Planning

1. Develop BU-level strategic initiatives
2. Define objectives, key results, and milestones
3. Identify resource requirements
4. Assess risks and dependencies
5. Submit proposals for portfolio review

### Phase 4: Integration & Alignment

1. Review and prioritize initiatives across portfolio
2. Allocate resources and resolve conflicts
3. Finalize strategic plan document
4. Prepare Board presentation materials

### Phase 5: Approval & Cascade

1. Present to Board for approval
2. Communicate approved strategy to organization
3. Translate to department and individual goals
4. Launch execution monitoring

## Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| CEO | Final accountability, Board presentation |
| CSO/Strategy | Process facilitation, synthesis, documentation |
| CFO | Financial modeling, resource allocation |
| BU Leaders | Unit-level planning and execution |
| Board | Approval and oversight |

## Deliverables

- Strategic Context Report
- Strategic Plan Document (3-5 year horizon)
- Annual Operating Plan
- Board Presentation Deck
- Cascade Communication Materials

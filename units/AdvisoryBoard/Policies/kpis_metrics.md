# Advisory Board (General) KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Advisory board performance indicators measuring strategic counsel quality, engagement effectiveness, and value delivery.

## Advisory Board KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Meeting Attendance | 80%+ | Per meeting | Board Liaison |
| Advice Implementation Rate | Tracked | Quarterly | Executive Sponsor |
| Advisor Engagement Score | Active | Quarterly | Board Liaison |
| Strategic Value Delivered | Documented | Annual | Executive Sponsor |
| Network Activation | Used for connections | Quarterly | Board Liaison |
| Advisor Retention | 80%+ | Annual | Board Liaison |
| Information Flow Quality | Two-way effective | Quarterly | Board Liaison |

## Engagement Metrics

### Meeting Effectiveness

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Advisor preparation | High | Per meeting |
| Discussion quality | Rich insights | Per meeting |
| Recommendations generated | Actionable | Per meeting |
| Follow-up completion | 100% | Monthly |

### Between-Meeting Engagement

- One-on-one consultations
- Email responsiveness
- Special project participation
- Informal network activation

## Value Delivery

| Area | Metric | Tracking |

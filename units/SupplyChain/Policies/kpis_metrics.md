# Supply Chain KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Supply chain performance indicators measuring end-to-end efficiency, inventory optimization, and logistics excellence.

## Supply Chain KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Perfect Order Rate | 98%+ | Monthly | SC Director |
| Inventory Turns | Industry benchmark+ | Monthly | Inventory Mgr |
| Order-to-Delivery Lead Time | Per product SLA | Weekly | Logistics |
| Supply Chain Cost (% of Rev) | Decreasing | Quarterly | SC Director |
| Supplier Fill Rate | 98%+ | Weekly | Supplier Mgmt |
| Forecast Accuracy | 85%+ | Monthly | Demand Planning |
| Cash-to-Cash Cycle | Decreasing | Monthly | Finance/SC |

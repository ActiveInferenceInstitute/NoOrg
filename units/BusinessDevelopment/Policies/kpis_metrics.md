# Business Development KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Business development performance indicators measuring partnership success, market expansion, and strategic growth.

## Business Development KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Partnership Pipeline Value | 3x annual target | Monthly | BD Director |
| Strategic Deals Closed | Per annual target | Quarterly | BD Director |
| New Market Revenue | Per plan | Quarterly | BD Manager |
| Partner-Sourced Revenue | 25%+ of new rev | Quarterly | Partnerships |
| Deal Conversion Rate | 30%+ | Monthly | BD Director |
| Partnership ROI | 3x investment | Annual | BD Director |
| Time to Partnership Value | <12 months | Per deal | BD Manager |

## Partnership Metrics

### Pipeline Health

- Opportunities by stage
- Pipeline velocity
- Win/loss analysis
- Partner type distribution (tech, channel, strategic)

### Partner Performance

| Partner Tier | Revenue Target | Engagement Level |
|--------------|----------------|------------------|
| Strategic | >$1M annually | Executive sponsor |
| Premier | >$500K annually | Director sponsor |
| Select | >$100K annually | Manager sponsor |
| Registered | Any | Self-service |

## Market Expansion

### New Market Entry

- Markets evaluated
- Go/no-go decisions
- Time to first revenue
- Market share within 24 months

### Channel Development

- New channel partners recruited
- Channel partner revenue
- Channel enablement completion
- Conflict resolution time

## Deal Metrics

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Average deal size | Growing | Quarterly |
| Deal cycle time | <90 days typical | Per deal |
| Contract negotiation time | <30 days | Per deal |
| Renewal rate | 90%+ | Annual |

## Relationship Metrics

### Executive Engagement

- C-level meetings held
- Joint planning sessions
- Executive business reviews
- Escalation frequency (lower is better)

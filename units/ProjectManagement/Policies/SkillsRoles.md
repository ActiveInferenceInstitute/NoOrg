# ProjectManagement Skills & Roles

## Overview

Required skills and role definitions for the ProjectManagement Unit.

## Core Competencies

### Technical Skills
- Domain expertise in ProjectManagement
- Tools and systems proficiency
- Data analysis and reporting

### Soft Skills
- Communication and collaboration
- Problem-solving
- Stakeholder management

## Role Requirements

### Director Level
- 10+ years experience
- Strategic planning expertise
- Leadership and mentoring

### Manager Level
- 5+ years experience
- Operational management
- Team leadership

### Specialist Level
- 3+ years experience
- Deep domain expertise
- Project execution

### Analyst Level
- 0-3 years experience
- Analytical skills
- Learning orientation

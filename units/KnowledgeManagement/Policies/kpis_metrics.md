# Knowledge Management KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Knowledge management performance indicators measuring knowledge capture, sharing, and organizational learning.

## KM KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Knowledge Base Usage | Growing 15% QoQ | Monthly | KM Manager |
| Content Contribution Rate | Per target | Monthly | KM Manager |
| Search Success Rate | 80%+ find needed info | Quarterly | KM Specialist |
| Time to Find Information | Decreasing | Quarterly | KM Manager |
| Knowledge Reuse Rate | Tracked | Quarterly | KM Manager |
| Expert Network Activity | Active participation | Monthly | CoP Lead |
| Knowledge Retention (Exits) | 100% critical captured | Per exit | KM Manager |

## Content Metrics

### Knowledge Base Health

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Articles created | Growing | Monthly |
| Articles updated | Within 12 months | Rolling |
| Stale content | <10% | Monthly |
| Accuracy rating | 95%+ | Quarterly |

### Content Quality

- User ratings (1-5 stars): Avg 4+
- Helpful votes
- Feedback incorporation rate
- Subject matter expert validation

## Usage Metrics

### Search Effectiveness

- Searches performed
- Click-through rate
- Zero-result searches: <5%
- Refinement rate (lower is better)

### Engagement

- Active users (monthly)
- Page views per user
- Repeat visitors
- Time on content

## Community of Practice (CoP)

### CoP Health

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Active CoPs | Per plan | Quarterly |
| Member participation | 30%+ active | Monthly |
| Events/sessions held | Per plan | Monthly |
| Cross-team collaboration | Tracked | Quarterly |

### Expert Network

- Experts registered
- Questions answered
- Response time
- Expertise coverage

## Business Impact

### Value Metrics

- Time saved through knowledge reuse
- Duplicate work avoided
- Faster problem resolution
- New employee ramp time reduction

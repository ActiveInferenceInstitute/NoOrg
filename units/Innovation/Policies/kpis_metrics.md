# Innovation KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Innovation performance indicators measuring ideation effectiveness, commercialization success, and organizational creativity.

## Innovation KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Ideas Submitted | Growing trend | Monthly | Innovation Mgr |
| Ideas Implemented | 10%+ of submitted | Quarterly | Innovation Mgr |
| Revenue from Innovation | 30%+ from <3yr products | Annual | Innovation Dir |
| Time to Proof of Concept | <90 days avg | Per project | PM |
| Innovation Portfolio Value | 5x investment | Quarterly | Innovation Dir |
| Patent Applications | Per annual target | Quarterly | IP Lead |
| Hackathon Participation | 25%+ of eligible employees | Per event | Innovation Mgr |

## Ideation Metrics

### Idea Pipeline

| Stage | Count | Conversion Rate |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| Ideas submitted | Track | Baseline |
| Ideas screened | Track | % of submitted |
| Concepts developed | Track | % of screened |
| Pilots launched | Track | % of concepts |
| Commercialized | Track | % of pilots |

- Ideas with business case: 80%+
- Cross-functional ideas: Growing
- Employee participation rate
- Repeat innovator rate

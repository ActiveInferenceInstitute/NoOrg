# Innovation Unit - Technical Documentation

## Overview

Drives R&D, experimental projects, and the incubation of new technologies.

The Innovation unit drives organizational transformation through systematic exploration of new ideas, technologies, and methodologies. It coordinates cross-functional innovation initiatives and manages the innovation pipeline from ideation through implementation.
## Directory Structure

```text
Innovation/
├── README.md        # Unit overview
├── Diagrams/        # Innovation process diagrams and architecture visualizations
├── MeetingNotes/    # Innovation meeting records and decisions
├── Policies/        # Innovation governance policies
├── Processes/       # Innovation lifecycle procedures
├── Projects/        # Active and completed project documentation
├── Reports/         # Innovation metrics and pipeline reports
└── Templates/       # Proposal, business case, and evaluation templates
```text

## Key Components

### Innovation Projects

Active and completed innovation project documentation including feasibility studies, proof-of-concept results, pilot program outcomes, and scaling assessments.

### Process Frameworks

Innovation methodologies (design thinking, lean startup, stage-gate), ideation and evaluation processes, rapid prototyping procedures, and innovation accounting frameworks.

### Templates

Standardized templates for innovation proposals, business case development, experiment design, and project documentation to ensure consistent evaluation criteria.

### Diagrams

Visual representations of innovation pipelines, technology roadmaps, and capability evolution plans.

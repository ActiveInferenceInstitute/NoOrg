# Human Resources KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

HR performance indicators measuring workforce effectiveness, talent management, and employee experience.

## HR Strategic KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Employee Engagement Score | 80%+ | Annual | HR Director |
| Voluntary Turnover Rate | <15% | Monthly | HR Analytics |
| Time to Fill | <45 days | Monthly | Talent Acquisition |
| Quality of Hire | 90%+ meet expectations at 1yr | Annual | TA Lead |
| Internal Mobility Rate | 25%+ | Quarterly | Talent Mgmt |
| Training Hours per Employee | 40+ hours/year | Quarterly | L&D |
| Diversity Representation | Per published goals | Quarterly | DEI Lead |

## Operational Metrics

### Talent Acquisition

- Applicants per opening
- Interview-to-offer ratio
- Offer acceptance rate
- Source of hire effectiveness
- Cost per hire

### Employee Experience

- Onboarding satisfaction: 90%+
- eNPS (Employee Net Promoter Score)
- Exit interview participation
- Manager effectiveness scores

### Compensation & Benefits

- Pay equity ratio
- Benefits utilization rate
- Market competitiveness ratio
- Total rewards satisfaction

### Learning & Development

- Training completion rate
- Skill gap closure rate
- Leadership pipeline strength
- Certification achievement

### Workforce Planning

- Headcount vs. plan
- Contractor ratio
- Succession coverage for critical roles
- Flight risk identification

## Compliance Metrics

| Requirement | Metric | Target |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| I-9 Compliance | Verification within 3 days | 100% |
| EEO Reporting | On-time submission | 100% |
| Training Compliance | Required training completion | 100% |
| Policy Acknowledgment | Annual acknowledgment | 100% |

## HR Dashboard

| Dashboard | Purpose | Audience |
|-----------|---------|----------|

| Talent Dashboard | Pipeline, hiring metrics | Talent Acquisition |
| Engagement Dashboard | Survey results, sentiment | All Managers |
| Compensation Dashboard | Pay analysis, equity | Comp Team |

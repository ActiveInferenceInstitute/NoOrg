# Human Resources Code of Conduct Policy

## 1. Purpose

This Code of Conduct Policy outlines the standards of professional behavior and ethical conduct expected of all employees while performing work for the organization, interacting with colleagues, customers, partners, and the public, and using company resources. It is intended to foster a respectful, ethical, safe, and productive work environment.

*(Note: If a comprehensive, organization-wide Code of Conduct exists elsewhere, this document might be simplified to primarily reference that central policy and perhaps add specific HR-related elaborations.)*

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees (full-time, part-time, temporary) of the organization, as well as contractors or consultants when interacting with employees or representing the organization.

## 3. Core Principles & Expectations

Employees are expected to conduct themselves professionally and ethically at all times, adhering to the following principles:

*   **Respect for Others:** Treat colleagues, customers, vendors, and all individuals with courtesy, dignity, and respect. Bullying, harassment, discrimination, or any form of abusive conduct is strictly prohibited. (See also [EEO & Anti-Harassment Policy](AGENTS.md)).
*   **Integrity & Honesty:** Act with honesty and integrity in all business dealings. Avoid misrepresentation, fraud, or unethical practices.
*   **Compliance with Laws & Policies:** Adhere to all applicable laws, regulations, and company policies, including those outlined in the [Employee Handbook](./EmployeeHandbook.md).
*   **Confidentiality:** Protect the organization's confidential information, as well as the private information of colleagues and customers. (See also [Data Privacy Policy (Employee)](AGENTS.md)).
*   **Conflict of Interest:** Avoid situations where personal interests conflict, or appear to conflict, with the interests of the organization. Disclose potential conflicts to your manager or HR.
*   **Use of Company Resources:** Use company assets (time, equipment, funds, information systems) responsibly and primarily for legitimate business purposes. Protect assets from loss, theft, or misuse.
*   **Professionalism:** Maintain a professional demeanor and appearance appropriate for the workplace (refer to Dress Code guidelines if applicable).
*   **Safety & Security:** Adhere to all safety and security procedures to ensure a safe working environment for everyone.

## 4. Specific Conduct Guidelines

*   **Attendance:** Adhere to work schedules and attendance policies.
*   **Work Performance:** Meet the performance standards and expectations of your role.
*   **Communication:** Communicate professionally and respectfully, whether verbally, in writing, or electronically.
*   **Drug & Alcohol Use:** Adhere to the organization's policy regarding alcohol and drug use in the workplace.
*   **Gifts & Entertainment:** Follow guidelines regarding the acceptance or offering of gifts and entertainment to avoid conflicts or the appearance of impropriety.
*   **Outside Employment:** [Specify policy regarding secondary employment, if any, especially concerning conflicts].

## 5. Reporting Concerns & Violations

Employees are encouraged and expected to report suspected violations of this Code, company policies, or applicable laws.
*   **Reporting Channels:** Concerns can typically be reported to:
    *   Your direct Manager
    *   Human Resources
    *   [Specific Ethics Hotline or Compliance Officer, if applicable]
*   **Non-Retaliation:** The organization strictly prohibits retaliation against any employee who reports a concern in good faith or participates in an investigation.

## 6. Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may lead to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment, depending on the severity and circumstances of the violation. Disciplinary actions will follow the procedures outlined in the [Disciplinary Action Policy](AGENTS.md).

## 7. Acknowledgment

All employees are required to acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to comply with this Code of Conduct Policy, typically upon hire and periodically thereafter.

## 8. Review

This policy will be reviewed periodically (typically annually) by HR, Legal, and Compliance (if applicable) to ensure its relevance and effectiveness.

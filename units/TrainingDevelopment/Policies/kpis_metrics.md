# Training & Development KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Training and development performance indicators measuring capability building, program effectiveness, and talent growth.

## T&D KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Training Investment per Employee | Per budget | Annual | T&D Director |
| Development Plan Completion | 90%+ | Annual | T&D Manager |
| High-Potential Retention | 95%+ | Annual | Talent Mgmt |
| Internal Promotion Rate | 60%+ of roles | Annual | T&D Director |
| Succession Coverage | 2+ per critical role | Quarterly | Talent Mgmt |
| Manager Effectiveness Score | 80%+ | Annual | Leadership Dev |
| Career Pathing Utilization | Growing | Quarterly | Career Dev |

## Program Effectiveness

### Leadership Development

| Program Level | Target Audience | Success Metric |
|---------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Emerging Leaders | Individual contributors | Promotion rate |
| New Managers | First-time managers | 360 improvement |
| Senior Leaders | Directors+ | Succession readiness |
| Executive | VPs+ | Board succession |

### Technical Development

- Skill certifications achieved
- Technical competency ratings
- Cross-training completion
- Subject matter expert growth

## Talent Metrics

### High-Potential Program

- HiPo identification rate: Per target
- HiPo development plan adherence
- HiPo retention: 95%+
- Acceleration outcomes

### Succession Planning

| Role Level | Coverage Target | Ready-Now |
|------------|-----------------|-----------|
| C-suite | 2-3 successors | 1+ |
| VP | 2 successors | 1 |
| Director | 2 successors | 1 |
| Critical roles | 1-2 successors | 1 |

## Career Development

### Career Pathing

- Career frameworks defined: 100% of roles
- Employee career plan adoption
- Internal mobility rate
- Career satisfaction score

### Mentoring

- Mentoring pairs active
- Mentee satisfaction: 90%+
- Mentor training completion
- Program retention impact

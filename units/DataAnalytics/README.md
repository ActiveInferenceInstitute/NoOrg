# Data Analytics Unit

## Overview

The Data Analytics Unit delivers actionable insights through data analysis, business intelligence, and advanced analytics.

## Mission

To enable data-driven decision making by transforming data into strategic insights.

## Key Responsibilities

- Business intelligence and reporting
- Data visualization and dashboards
- Predictive analytics and modeling
- Data governance and quality
- Self-service analytics enablement

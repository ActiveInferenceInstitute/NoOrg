# Organizational Development KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Organizational development performance indicators measuring change effectiveness, culture health, and organizational capability.

## OD KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Change Initiative Success Rate | 80%+ | Per initiative | OD Director |
| Employee Engagement Score | 80%+ | Annual | OD Director |
| Culture Alignment Score | 85%+ | Annual | Culture Lead |
| Organizational Agility | Top quartile | Annual | OD Director |
| Team Effectiveness Score | 80%+ | Quarterly | Team Dev Lead |
| Leadership Effectiveness | 80%+ | Annual | Leadership Dev |
| Change Adoption Rate | Per initiative target | Per change | Change Lead |

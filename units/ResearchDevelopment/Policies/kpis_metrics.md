# Research & Development KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

R&D performance indicators measuring innovation output, development efficiency, and strategic value creation.

## R&D KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| New Product Revenue % | 30%+ of total revenue | Annual | R&D VP |
| Time to Market | Decreasing trend | Per release | PM |
| R&D Spend Efficiency | Revenue/R&D spend growing | Quarterly | Finance |
| Patent Filing Rate | Per annual target | Quarterly | IP Lead |
| Innovation Pipeline Value | 5x R&D investment | Quarterly | R&D VP |
| Technical Debt Ratio | <20% of backlog | Sprint | Engineering |
| Research Publication Rate | Per target | Annual | Research Lead |

## Innovation Metrics

### Pipeline Health

- Ideas in discovery
- Concepts in validation
- Products in development
- Products launched YTD

### Stage-Gate Performance

| Stage | Average Duration | Pass Rate |
|-------|-----------------|-----------|
| Discovery | 2 months | 40% |
| Concept | 3 months | 60% |
| Development | 6 months | 80% |
| Validation | 2 months | 90% |
| Launch | 1 month | 95% |

## Development Metrics

### Engineering Velocity

- Story points completed per sprint
- Velocity trend
- Sprint burndown consistency
- Release frequency

### Quality

- Bug escape rate: <5%
- Code coverage: 80%+
- Technical debt reduction
- Security vulnerabilities resolved

### Efficiency

- Developer productivity
- Rework percentage: <15%
- CI/CD pipeline efficiency
- Environment availability

## Research Metrics

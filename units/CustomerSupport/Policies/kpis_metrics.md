# Customer Support KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Customer support performance indicators measuring service quality, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

## Support KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) | 90%+ | Per interaction | Support Dir |
| Net Promoter Score (NPS) | 50+ | Quarterly | CX Lead |
| First Contact Resolution (FCR) | 75%+ | Monthly | Support Mgr |
| Average Handle Time (AHT) | Per SLA by type | Daily | Team Lead |
| Customer Effort Score (CES) | Low effort | Quarterly | CX Lead |
| SLA Compliance | 95%+ | Monthly | Support Ops |
| Ticket Backlog | Decreasing | Daily | Support Mgr |

## Response Time SLAs

| Priority | First Response | Resolution | Target Compliance |
|----------|---------------|------------|-------------------|
| Critical (P1) | 15 minutes | 4 hours | 99% |
| High (P2) | 1 hour | 8 hours | 95% |
| Medium (P3) | 4 hours | 24 hours | 90% |
| Low (P4) | 8 hours | 72 hours | 85% |

## Volume Metrics

### Ticket Analysis

- Total tickets opened/closed
- Tickets by channel (phone, email, chat, self-service)
- Tickets by category/product
- Ticket escalation rate: <10%

### Self-Service Adoption

- Knowledge base views
- Self-service resolution rate: 40%+
- Chatbot containment rate: 60%+
- Community engagement

## Agent Performance

| Metric | Target | Coaching Trigger |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| Tickets handled per day | Per role | <80% avg |
| Quality score | 90%+ | <85% |
| Schedule adherence | 95%+ | <90% |
| Customer satisfaction | 90%+ | <85% |
| First contact resolution | 75%+ | <65% |

## Quality Metrics

### Quality Assurance

- QA score average: 90%+
- Calls monitored per agent: 5/month
- Calibration variance: <5%
- Coaching effectiveness

### Issue Resolution

- Reopen rate: <5%
- Escalation rate: <10%
- Known issue resolution time
- Root cause analysis completion

## Channel Performance

| Channel | Volume % | Target Metrics |
|---------|----------|----------------|
| Phone | 30% | AHT <8 min, CSAT 90% |
| Email | 25% | Response <4 hr, CSAT 88% |
| Chat | 30% | Response <1 min, CSAT 92% |
| Self-Service | 15% | Deflection 40%+ |

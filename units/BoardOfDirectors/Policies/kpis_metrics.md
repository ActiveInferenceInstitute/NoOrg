# Board of Directors KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Board performance indicators measuring governance effectiveness, fiduciary oversight, and strategic stewardship.

## Board KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Board Meeting Attendance | 90%+ | Per meeting | Corporate Secretary |
| Fiduciary Duty Compliance | 100% | Continuous | General Counsel |
| Strategic Plan Oversight | Quarterly reviews | Quarterly | Board Chair |
| Risk Oversight Effectiveness | Active engagement | Quarterly | Risk Committee |
| CEO Performance Review | Completed annually | Annual | Comp Committee |
| Board Self-Assessment | Action items addressed | Annual | Nom/Gov Committee |
| Shareholder Value Creation | Outperform peers | Annual | Full Board |

- Materials distributed: 5+ days in advance
- Meeting productivity score

## Committee Performance

### Audit Committee

- Financial statement review: Each quarter
- External auditor oversight: Active
- Internal audit review: Quarterly
- Whistleblower oversight: Established

### Compensation Committee

- Executive compensation review: Annual
- Say-on-pay preparation: Annual
- Pay-for-performance alignment: Verified
- Peer benchmarking: Annual

### Nominating/Governance

- Director recruitment: Per succession plan
- Board evaluation: Annual
- Governance guideline review: Annual
- ESG oversight: Established

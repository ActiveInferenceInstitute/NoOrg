# MetaGovernance KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Meta-governance performance indicators measuring governance framework effectiveness, policy coherence, and oversight quality.

## MetaGovernance KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Governance Framework Coverage | 100% | Annual | MetaGov Director |
| Policy Coherence Score | 95%+ | Quarterly | Policy Lead |
| Oversight Mechanism Effectiveness | High rated | Annual | MetaGov Director |
| Decision Transparency | Per framework | Continuous | Governance Lead |
| Stakeholder Representation | Balanced | Quarterly | Stakeholder Lead |
| Governance Maturity Level | Advancing | Annual | MetaGov Director |
| Inter-Unit Coordination | Effective | Quarterly | Coordination Lead |

## Governance Framework Metrics

### Framework Health

| Element | Target | Assessment |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Policies defined | Complete | Annual |
| Procedures documented | Complete | Annual |
| Roles assigned | 100% | Continuous |
| Accountability clear | 100% | Continuous |

### Policy Management

- Policies current: 100%
- Policy review cycle: Adhered
- Policy conflicts: Zero
- Policy gaps: Identified and addressed

## Oversight Effectiveness

### Committee Structure

- Committee charters: Current
- Committee composition: Appropriate
- Meeting frequency: Per charter
- Decision documentation: Complete

### Escalation Quality

| Escalation Type | Target Response | Resolution |
|-----------------|-----------------|------------|
| Policy conflict | 24 hours | 5 days |
| Authority dispute | 48 hours | 10 days |
| Framework gap | 72 hours | 30 days |

- Transparency rating

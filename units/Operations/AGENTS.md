# Operations Unit - Technical Documentation

## Overview

Optimizes core business processes, resource allocation, and workflow efficiency.

The Operations unit ensures organizational efficiency through standardized processes, resource optimization, and continuous workflow improvement. It coordinates across all operational functions to maintain service delivery and operational excellence.

```text

├── MeetingNotes/    # Operations meeting records and decisions

```text

## Document Types

### Operations Policies

Operational standards, efficiency guidelines, resource management policies, business continuity procedures, and capacity planning frameworks.

### Operations Processes

Day-to-day operational procedures, workflow optimization protocols, resource allocation methods, change management processes, and escalation procedures.

### Reports

Operational KPI dashboards, efficiency analysis reports, capacity utilization metrics, SLA compliance tracking, and continuous improvement findings.

# Marketing Unit Charter

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01
**Approved By:** Executive Committee

## 1. Purpose

The Marketing unit is established to provide strategic and operational excellence in marketing-related matters, supporting organizational objectives and stakeholder needs.

## 2. Mission

To deliver high-quality marketing services that enable organizational success through expert guidance, efficient processes, and continuous improvement.

## 3. Vision

To be recognized as a best-in-class marketing function that drives value and innovation across the organization.

## 4. Scope

### In Scope
- Marketing strategy development and execution
- Policy development and maintenance
- Process design and optimization
- Stakeholder support and guidance
- Compliance and quality assurance

### Out of Scope
- Activities clearly owned by other units
- External regulatory enforcement
- Direct budget authority over other units

## 5. Core Functions

1. **Strategic Planning**: Develop and maintain marketing strategy
2. **Policy Management**: Create and update policies and procedures
3. **Operations**: Execute core marketing activities
4. **Advisory**: Provide expert guidance to stakeholders
5. **Reporting**: Deliver insights and performance metrics

## 6. Governance

- Reports to: Executive Leadership
- Reviews: Quarterly with Executive Committee
- Policies: Aligned with Governance unit standards

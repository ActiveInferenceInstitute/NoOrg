# Marketing KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Marketing performance indicators measuring brand awareness, demand generation, and revenue contribution.

## Marketing KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) | Per quarterly target | Weekly | Demand Gen |
| MQL to SQL Conversion | 25%+ | Monthly | Marketing Ops |
| Cost per Lead (CPL) | Decreasing trend | Monthly | Marketing Ops |
| Marketing Sourced Pipeline | 30%+ of total | Quarterly | CMO |
| Marketing Influenced Revenue | 50%+ of closed | Quarterly | CMO |
| Brand Awareness | Growing % | Quarterly | Brand |
| Website Traffic | 15% growth QoQ | Monthly | Digital |

## Demand Generation Metrics

### Lead Metrics

- Total leads generated
- Leads by source (paid, organic, referral, events)
- Lead quality score distribution
- Lead-to-opportunity conversion by source

### Campaign Performance

- Campaign ROI
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Email open rate: 20%+
- Email click rate: 3%+
- Landing page conversion: 5%+

### Content Marketing

- Content downloads
- Time on page
- Content attribution to pipeline
- Organic search rankings

## Digital Marketing Metrics

| Channel | Key Metrics | Target |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Paid Search | CTR, CPC, Conversions | CTR >3% |
| Paid Social | Engagement, CPL | CPL <$50 |
| SEO | Rankings, Traffic, Leads | Top 10 for targets |
| Email | Open rate, CTR, Unsubscribes | <0.5% unsub |
| Website | Sessions, Bounce rate, Conversions | <50% bounce |

## Brand Metrics

### Awareness

- Aided/unaided brand awareness
- Share of voice vs. competitors
- Media mentions and sentiment
- Social media followers and engagement

### Perception

- Net Promoter Score contribution
- Brand attribute tracking
- Customer satisfaction with brand

## Attribution Model

| Model | Use Case | Weighting |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| First Touch | Awareness measurement | 100% first |
| Last Touch | Conversion credit | 100% last |
| Linear | Equal distribution | Equal all |
| Time Decay | Recent emphasis | Weighted recent |
| Data-Driven | ML-based | Algorithmic |

## Dashboards

| Dashboard | Purpose | Audience |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Marketing Performance | Overall metrics, trends | CMO/Leadership |
| Demand Gen Dashboard | Leads, campaigns, funnel | Demand Gen |
| Digital Dashboard | Channel performance | Digital Team |
| Content Dashboard | Asset performance | Content Team |

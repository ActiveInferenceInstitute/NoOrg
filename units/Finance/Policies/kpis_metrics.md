# Finance KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Financial performance indicators and metrics driving fiscal health and operational excellence.

## Financial KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Revenue Growth | 15% YoY | Quarterly | CFO |
| Operating Margin | 20%+ | Monthly | Controller |
| Cash Flow Ratio | >1.2 | Monthly | Treasury |
| DSO (Days Sales Outstanding) | <45 days | Monthly | AR Manager |
| DPO (Days Payable Outstanding) | 30-45 days | Monthly | AP Manager |
| Budget Variance | ±5% | Monthly | FP&A |
| Working Capital Ratio | 1.5-2.0 | Monthly | Treasury |
| Return on Equity (ROE) | 15%+ | Quarterly | CFO |

## Operational Metrics

### Accounts Receivable

- Collection rate: 98%+
- Aging bucket distribution (0-30, 31-60, 61-90, 90+)
- Bad debt write-offs: <1% of revenue

### Accounts Payable

- Invoice processing time: <3 business days
- Early payment discount capture: 80%+
- Payment accuracy: 99.9%

### Financial Close

- Monthly close cycle: 5 business days
- Quarterly close cycle: 10 business days
- Reconciliation accuracy: 100%

### Budgeting & Forecasting

- Forecast accuracy: ±3%
- Budget cycle completion: On schedule
- Variance analysis turnaround: 2 days post-close

# Finance Compliance Requirements

## Overview

Regulatory and internal compliance obligations governing financial operations.

## Regulatory Framework

| Regulation | Description | Applicability |
|------------|-------------|---------------|
| SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley) | Internal controls over financial reporting | Public companies |
| GAAP | Generally Accepted Accounting Principles | All entities |
| IFRS | International Financial Reporting Standards | International ops |
| SEC Regulations | Securities reporting requirements | Public companies |
| Tax Compliance | Federal, state, local tax obligations | All entities |
| ASC 606 | Revenue recognition standard | Revenue contracts |

## Internal Controls

### Financial Controls

- Segregation of duties between authorization, custody, and recording
- Dual approval for transactions >$10,000
- Management review of journal entries
- Bank reconciliation by independent party

### IT General Controls

- Access management for financial systems
- Change management for ERP modifications
- Data backup and recovery procedures
- System security and encryption

### Entity-Level Controls

- Tone at the top / Code of Ethics
- Whistleblower program
- Fraud risk assessment
- Internal audit function

## Audit Requirements

| Audit Type | Scope | Frequency | Auditor |
|------------|-------|-----------|---------|
| External Financial Audit | Financial statements | Annual | Big 4 / External |
| SOX 404 Audit | Internal controls | Annual | External + Internal |
| Internal Audit | Process compliance | Quarterly | Internal Audit |
| Tax Audit | Tax positions | As required | Tax Authority |

## Key Controls Matrix

| Process | Key Control | Control Type | Testing Frequency |
|---------|-------------|--------------|-------------------|
| Revenue | Contract review | Preventive | Quarterly |
| Expenditure | PO matching | Preventive | Monthly |
| Payroll | Timesheet approval | Preventive | Per cycle |
| Treasury | Bank rec review | Detective | Monthly |
| Close | JE approval | Preventive | Monthly |

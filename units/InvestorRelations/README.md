# Investor Relations Unit

## Overview

The Investor Relations Unit manages communications with the investment community to support fair valuation and access to capital.

## Mission

To build trust with shareholders and analysts through transparent, accurate, and timely financial communications.

## Key Responsibilities

- Earnings announcements and investor calls
- Annual and quarterly report production
- Investor presentations and conferences
- Analyst relations management
- Regulatory disclosure compliance

# Investor Relations KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

IR performance indicators measuring shareholder engagement, market perception, and communication effectiveness.

## IR KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Analyst Coverage | 10+ active analysts | Quarterly | IR Director |
| Earnings Call Participation | 50+ participants | Quarterly | IR Director |
| Investor Meeting Volume | 200+ per year | Annual | IRO |
| Consensus Accuracy | Actuals within 5% | Quarterly | IR Director |
| Shareholder Engagement | Top 50 engaged | Annual | IRO |
| IR Website Traffic | Growing 10% YoY | Quarterly | IR Specialist |
| Recognition Awards | Industry top 10 | Annual | IR Director |

## Shareholder Metrics

### Ownership Analysis

- Institutional ownership %
- Top 20 shareholder concentration
- Geographic distribution
- Investment style breakdown (growth, value, index)

### Shareholder Engagement

- Meetings by shareholder tier
- Roadshow participation
- Conference attendance
- Perception study results

## Market Perception

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Stock price performance | Outperform index | Daily |
| Valuation multiple | Premium to peers | Quarterly |
| Trading volume | Healthy liquidity | Daily |
| Short interest | Below sector avg | Monthly |

## Communication Effectiveness

### Earnings Releases

- On-time release: 100%
- Media pickup rate
- Analyst report turnaround
- Stock price reaction (event study)

### Regulatory Filings

- 10-K/10-Q on-time: 100%
- 8-K timeliness: 100%
- Form 4 compliance: 100%
- Proxy statement clarity score

## Perception Studies

| Accessibility | 90%+ positive | Annual |

# InvestorRelations Reports

## Overview

Standard reporting templates and documentation for the InvestorRelations Unit.

## Report Types

### Status Reports
Monthly/quarterly status updates on InvestorRelations activities.

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Executive Summary | Key highlights and concerns |
| Progress | Completed items and milestones |
| Metrics | KPIs and measurements |
| Risks | Current risk status |
| Next Steps | Upcoming priorities |

### Performance Reports
Analysis of InvestorRelations effectiveness and outcomes.

### Stakeholder Reports
External-facing summary for leadership and stakeholders.

## Report Schedule

| Report | Frequency | Audience |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Weekly Status | Weekly | Team |
| Monthly Report | Monthly | Management |
| Quarterly Review | Quarterly | Leadership |
| Annual Report | Annually | Board |

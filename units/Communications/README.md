# Communications Unit

## Overview

The Communications Unit manages all internal and external organizational communications, ensuring consistent messaging and effective stakeholder engagement.

## Mission

To deliver clear, timely, and impactful communications that support organizational objectives and strengthen stakeholder relationships.

## Key Responsibilities

- Internal employee communications
- External public relations and media relations
- Crisis communications management
- Brand voice and messaging consistency
- Executive communications support

# Communications KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Communications performance indicators measuring message reach, engagement, and brand reputation.

## Communications KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Media Mentions | Growing 20% YoY | Monthly | Media Relations |
| Share of Voice | Top 3 in sector | Quarterly | Comms Director |
| Message Penetration | 80%+ recall | Quarterly | Comms Director |
| Internal Comms Read Rate | 75%+ | Per communication | Internal Comms |
| Crisis Response Time | <1 hour initial | Per incident | Comms Director |
| Social Media Engagement | Growing 15% QoQ | Monthly | Social Media |
| Employee Advocacy Score | 70%+ | Quarterly | Internal Comms |

## External Communications

### Media Relations

- Press releases issued per quarter
- Media pickup rate: 60%+
- Tier 1 placements
- Journalist relationship score
- Message pull-through accuracy

### Public Relations

- Speaking opportunities secured
- Industry awards nominations/wins
- Thought leadership articles published
- Analyst/influencer briefings

## Internal Communications

| Channel | Metric | Target |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Intranet | Page views, engagement | Growing |
| Email (All-hands) | Open rate | 70%+ |
| Town Hall | Attendance | 80%+ |
| Manager Cascade | Completion rate | 100% |

### Employee Engagement

- Communication satisfaction score
- Information accessibility rating
- Two-way dialogue effectiveness
- Change communication success

## Digital & Social

### Social Media Metrics

- Followers/audience growth
- Engagement rate: 3%+
- Content performance by type
- Sentiment analysis: 80%+ positive

### Website/Blog

- Traffic and page views
- Time on page
- Content downloads
- SEO performance

## Crisis Communications

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Response time (initial) | <1 hour | Per incident |
| Message consistency | 100% | Per incident |
| Stakeholder notification | Per plan | Per incident |
| Post-crisis sentiment | Recovery within 30 days | Per incident |

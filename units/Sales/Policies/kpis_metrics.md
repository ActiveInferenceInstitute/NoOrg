# Sales KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Sales performance indicators measuring revenue generation, pipeline health, and team effectiveness.

## Sales KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Revenue vs. Quota | 100%+ | Monthly | Sales VP |
| Win Rate | 25%+ | Monthly | Sales Ops |
| Average Deal Size | Growing 10% YoY | Quarterly | Sales VP |
| Sales Cycle Length | Decreasing trend | Monthly | Sales Ops |
| Pipeline Coverage | 3x+ | Weekly | Sales Mgrs |
| Quota Attainment (% of reps) | 70%+ | Monthly | Sales VP |
| Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) | Decreasing trend | Quarterly | Finance |

## Pipeline Metrics

### Pipeline Health

- Total pipeline value by stage
- Pipeline velocity (deals moved per week)
- Stage conversion rates
- Pipeline aging (deals >90 days)

### Opportunity Management

- Opportunities created
- Opportunities Won/Lost
- Lost reason analysis
- Competitive win rate

## Revenue Metrics

### Bookings

- New business bookings
- Expansion bookings
- Renewal bookings
- ARR/MRR growth

### Customer Metrics

- Logo count
- Net Revenue Retention: 110%+
- Gross Revenue Retention: 90%+
- Churn rate: <10%

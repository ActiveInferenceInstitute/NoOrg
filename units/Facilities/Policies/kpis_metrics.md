# Facilities KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Facilities management performance indicators measuring space efficiency, operational excellence, and workplace experience.

## Facilities KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Space Utilization | 70-80% | Monthly | FM Director |
| Cost per Square Foot | Benchmark competitive | Quarterly | FM Director |
| Work Order Completion | 95%+ within SLA | Weekly | FM Manager |
| Energy Efficiency | 10% YoY reduction | Monthly | Sustainability |
| Workplace Satisfaction | 85%+ | Quarterly | Workplace Exp |
| Uptime (Critical Systems) | 99.9%+ | Continuous | Engineering |
| Safety Incidents | Zero | Monthly | EHS |

## Space Management

### Utilization Metrics

- Desk occupancy rate
- Meeting room utilization
- Collaborative space usage
- Vacant space tracking

### Capacity Planning

- Headcount vs. capacity ratio
- Growth accommodation runway
- Flexible space ratio
- Remote work impact

## Maintenance Metrics

| Maintenance Type | Target Response | Target Resolution |
|------------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Emergency (safety) | 15 minutes | 2 hours |
| Urgent (system down) | 1 hour | 8 hours |
| Routine | 24 hours | 5 days |
| Preventive | Scheduled | Per schedule |

### Preventive Maintenance

- PM compliance rate: 100%
- Equipment uptime: 99%+
- Asset lifecycle management
- Vendor SLA compliance

## Sustainability Metrics

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Energy consumption (kWh/SF) | Decreasing | Monthly |
| Water usage (gal/SF) | Decreasing | Monthly |
| Waste diversion rate | 80%+ | Monthly |
| LEED certification status | Maintain/upgrade | Annual |
| Carbon footprint | Reduction path | Annual |

## Workplace Experience

### Employee Satisfaction

- Overall workplace score
- Temperature/comfort rating
- Cleanliness rating
- Amenities satisfaction
- Technology/tools rating

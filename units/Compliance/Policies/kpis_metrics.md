# Compliance KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Compliance performance indicators measuring regulatory adherence, program effectiveness, and risk mitigation.

## Compliance KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Regulatory Findings | Zero critical | Per exam | CCO |
| Policy Compliance Rate | 98%+ | Quarterly | Compliance Mgr |
| Training Completion | 100% | Per program | Training Lead |
| Issue Remediation | 100% on time | Monthly | Compliance Mgr |
| Third-Party Compliance | 95%+ | Annual | Vendor Mgmt |
| Whistleblower Response | 100% within SLA | Per report | CCO |
| Regulatory Filing Accuracy | 100% | Per filing | Regulatory |

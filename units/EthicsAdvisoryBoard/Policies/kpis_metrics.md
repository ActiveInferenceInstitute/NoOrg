# Ethics Advisory Board KPIs and Metrics

## Overview

Ethics advisory board performance indicators measuring ethical guidance quality, organizational integrity, and stakeholder trust.

## EAB KPIs

| KPI | Target | Frequency | Owner |
|-----|--------|-----------|-------|
| Ethics Policy Review | Annual update | Annual | Chief Ethics Officer |
| Ethical Dilemma Consultation | Timely response | As needed | EAB Chair |
| Whistleblower Oversight | Active engagement | Quarterly | EAB Liaison |
| AI Ethics Guidance | Proactive input | Quarterly | EAB Chair |
| Research Ethics Review | Thorough | Per protocol | EAB Liaison |
| Stakeholder Trust Metrics | Improving | Annual | CEO |
| Regulatory Ethics Compliance | 100% | Continuous | CLO |

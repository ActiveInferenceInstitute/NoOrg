Organization Details

Organization: Sustainable Urban Agriculture Technologies Institute

Description: A compact research organization focused on advancing sustainable urban agriculture technologies.

Units: 4

Total Roles: 8

Units & Roles

Research Unit

Responsible for conducting primary research and feasibility studies on urban agriculture technologies.

Roles:

Lead Researcher

Oversees all research activities and ensures alignment with organizational goals.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop research agendas and methodologies
  • Supervise research team members
  • Analyze and interpret research data
Research Analyst

Conducts data collection and analysis for research projects.

Responsibilities:

  • Collect data from various sources
  • Perform statistical analyses
  • Prepare research reports

Development Unit

Focuses on the engineering and prototyping of new urban agriculture technologies.

Roles:

Development Manager

Leads the development of prototypes and ensures technical feasibility.

Responsibilities:

  • Plan and oversee development projects
  • Coordinate with research and business units
  • Evaluate prototypes for efficiency and sustainability
Engineer

Designs and tests prototypes for urban agriculture technologies.

Responsibilities:

  • Create design specifications
  • Build and test prototypes
  • Document engineering processes

Community Outreach Unit

Engages with communities to promote urban agriculture technologies and gather feedback.

Roles:

Outreach Coordinator

Plans and executes community engagement activities.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop outreach programs
  • Coordinate with local communities
  • Collect community feedback
Community Engagement Specialist

Facilitates workshops and provides support for community projects.

Responsibilities:

  • Conduct community workshops
  • Provide technical assistance to community members
  • Evaluate community impact

Business Development Unit

Responsible for the commercialization of technologies and securing funding.

Roles:

Business Development Manager

Identifies market opportunities and manages partnerships.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop business strategies
  • Establish partnerships with stakeholders
  • Secure funding and investments
Marketing Specialist

Promotes the organization's technologies and initiatives.

Responsibilities:

  • Create marketing materials
  • Manage social media presence
  • Organize promotional events
